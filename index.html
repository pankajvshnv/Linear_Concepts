Linear Concepts is an Architectural, Interior and Urban-Designing firm with a reputation for challenging convention and pursuing design excellence. We work in an intimate, responsive environment that collectively values good design, simplicity and positive relationships.

We've worked on over 350 projects pan-India since 1988 — spanning High Rise Housing, Retail, Corporate Interiors, Housing Development, High-End Villas, Hotels and Resorts.
